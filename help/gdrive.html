☁️ Google Drive Cloud

This page allows you to save your data to Google Drive so you never lose it.

🔗 CONNECTION: Sign in with your Google account. A dedicated folder named 'Memoria Backups' will be created. Do not delete it manually.

🔢 BACKUP COUNT: You can decide how many versions of the database to keep. Once the limit is reached, the app will overwrite the oldest file.

⚡ IMMEDIATE BACKUP: Send your current data to the cloud immediately. You have a maximum of 3 immediate backups on Drive.

🔄 RESTORE: Download data from the cloud to your phone. WARNING: Current data on the phone will be replaced by the downloaded files.

🔒 PRIVACY: The app only accesses its own folder and cannot read your personal files on Drive.

🛡️ SECURITY NOTE (Unverified App)

During login, Google might show a warning defining the app as "unverified". This happens because the app is an independent project and does not have a certified corporate web domain. It does not indicate a risk to your data.
