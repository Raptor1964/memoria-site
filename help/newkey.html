🔐 Encryption and Security

On this page, you can manage the protection of your data. The encryption key ensures that only you can access your content:

🔑 CHANGE KEY: By entering a new key and saving, the app will immediately update the protection system. Choose a strong key that you can easily remember.

⚠️ BACKUP COMPATIBILITY: Warning: by changing the key, all old backup copies created previously will become unusable.

After saving, remember to generate and replace your old backups using the new key.

🛡️ ABSOLUTE PRIVACY: Memoria does not store your key on external servers. Your privacy is total, but we cannot recover your data if you lose your access key.

💾 SAVING: Once the new key is entered, press the "Save" button to confirm. The application will apply the changes in a few moments.
