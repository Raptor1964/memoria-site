🏠 Account List

On this screen, you have full control over your stored data:

📊 COUNTER: At the top right, you can see the total number of entries currently stored in your database.

📂 SERVICE LIST: Each item in the list shows an icon (custom or default) and the name of the service.

  • Single tap: Clicking on the name or icon will take you to the details page, where you can view passwords, notes, and other info.

⚙️ SETTINGS: Located in the bottom left bar, this button allows you to customize the app, change the theme, or manage your user account.

➕ ADD ENTRY: Located in the bottom right bar, this is the quick shortcut for adding a new service to the database.
