🌍 Language Settings

On this page, you can customize the Memoria interface by choosing your preferred language:

📱 SYSTEM LANGUAGE: By selecting this option, the app will automatically adapt to the language set on your smartphone.

🌐 MANUAL CHOICE: You can choose from a wide range of supported languages (English, Italian, French, German, and many others).

🤝 COLLABORATION: Translations are maintained by the community. If you find errors or want to help us improve a translation, you can contact us at the email address listed in the Play Store.

🙏 ACKNOWLEDGMENTS: Under each language, you will find the name of the collaborator who made the translation possible for that language.
