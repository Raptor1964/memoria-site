🎨 Theme Customization

Adapt Memoria's visual appearance to your taste or ambient lighting conditions:

⚙️ SYSTEM THEME: By activating this option, the app will automatically follow your device settings (light/dark) based on the time of day or your global preferences.

☀️ LIGHT THEMES: If you prefer a bright interface, you can choose variants like 'Spring' or 'Ylenia'. They offer high contrast, ideal for outdoor use.

🌙 DARK THEME: Transform the interface to rest your eyes. It is perfect for nighttime use and helps reduce battery consumption on OLED screens.

🔄 INSTANT CHANGE: No need to restart the app. By selecting an option via the buttons, the colors will change instantly before your eyes.
