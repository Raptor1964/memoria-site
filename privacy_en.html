Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This policy describes the processing of personal data carried out by the Memoria application, developed by Giancarlo De Rosa.

1. Data Collection and Purpose

The application is designed to operate primarily in local mode. However, it collects the following data for specific purposes:

  • Email Address: Used exclusively upon user request for the app access password recovery service.
  • Google Account: Required to enable the optional backup feature on Google Drive.

2. Use of Google Drive (OAuth Scope)

The app requests access to Google Drive APIs using the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scope.

What this means: The app can only view, create, and manage files and folders that it has created itself. It has no access to any of your other personal files stored on Drive.

3. Account Recovery Service

If the user forgets their access password, they may request their credentials to be sent via email. It is the user's obligation to change the password immediately after the first successful login to restore the security of the local database.

4. Retention and Security

  • Saved account data is encrypted locally on the device.
  • Email addresses and technical transmission data are deleted periodically (never kept longer than 90 days).
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties.
